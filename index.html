Biography

I am currently a Special Appointed Assistant Professor at  Aonishi Lab,  The University of Tokyo. I was a Specially-Appointed Researcher/Fellow at  ISLab,  The University of Osaka. I obtained my Ph.D. degree at  Takemura Lab,  The University of Osaka, advised by Prof. Haruo Takemura and Prof. Photchara Ratsamee. I was a visiting scholar at  Georgia Tech advised by Prof. Animesh Garg. I was a specially appointed researcher at  Sysmex Corporation, and embedded software engineer worked at   Huawei,     ZTE Corporation, etc.

Research interests: Multimodal Large Language Model, Embodied AI, Affective Computing, and Explainable AI.

Openings: I am open to receiving all kinds and forms of collaboration opportunities related to my research interests!
